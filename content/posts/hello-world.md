Welcome to my blog. This is a simple, SEO-friendly site built with Next.js.

## Why this stack?

- **Next.js** – Server-side rendering for great SEO and fast loads
- **Markdown** – Write posts in simple files, no database needed
- **Light & dark mode** – Easy on the eyes any time of day

## What's next?

Add more `.md` files in `content/posts/` with frontmatter like:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Post Title"
date: "2025-02-25"
description: "A short description for SEO."
---
```

Then write your content below. That's it!
